About

  • Yoko's Kitchen offers authentic Japanese cooking classes in a warm and welcoming environment.

  • Our goal is to share the traditions, techniques, and flavors of Japanese home cooking with food lovers of all skill levels.

  • Led by experienced instructors, our classes focus on fresh ingredients, simple methods, and beautifully balanced dishes.

  • Whether you are a beginner or an enthusiastic home cook, you will learn how to prepare classic Japanese recipes with confidence.

  • Join us to discover the art of Japanese cuisine, enjoy hands-on cooking experiences, and share delicious meals together.

  • At Yoko's Kitchen, cooking is not just about food - it's about culture, creativity, and community.

Popular Recipes

Yakitori (grilled chicken) Tsukune (minced meat patties) Okonomiyaki (savory pancakes) Mizutaki (chicken stew)
