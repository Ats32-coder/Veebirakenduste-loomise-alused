Soups

Warm, comforting, and full of delicate flavors — Japanese soups are built on rich broths and fresh seasonal ingredients. In our classes, you will learn how to prepare balanced, nourishing soups that highlight the essence of traditional Japanese cooking.

Steaks

Tender, perfectly seasoned, and beautifully presented — our steak dishes combine Japanese techniques with premium ingredients. Discover how to achieve the ideal texture, flavor balance, and elegant plating that define Japanese-style meat dishes.
